Campus Life

School Library

Was established in 1978

The school has three libraries (Junior, Middle and Senior) which are very well stocked with more than 20835 books – fiction, non-fiction, autobiographies, biographies, subject-related encyclopedias, etc. Students spend quality time undertaking reference work, selecting their favourite author, browsing through new titles and updating themselves through a host of journals and magazines covering commerce, finance, science, general knowledge, politics, nature, art, sports and entertainment. The senior library has been fully revamped with brand new furniture, computers for reference work and has also been air-conditioned to provide children with a quiet and comfortable environment for reference work. A newly constructed air-conditioned Middle School library with aesthetic furniture is a favourite place for the bookworms of the middle school. To reach out to classrooms, the junior school has initiated classroom libraries for Classes I and II which consist of fiction and non-fiction books. These libraries are utilised by students under the supervision of the Class Teacher. This is in addition to the dedicated Junior School library.



School Auditorium

Was established in 1978

An air-conditioned auditorium with a seating capacity of 200 people is used for school functions and festive occasions. An air-conditioned audio-visual hall is available for students for debates, lectures, discussions, seminars, group classes etc. The hall is equipped for 120 people, and is also smart-class enabled with a computer, projector, interactive board, mics and speakers.

Achievments

OVERALL TROPHY- ‘THE CYBER EVENT’ AT JAGRAN PUBLIC SCHOOL
Came first in inter house debate competition

The IT students of The Khaitan School participated in Interschool Computer Fest “Battle of Titans” held at Mayoor School, Noida and brought home the Overall Trophy. Out of 40 participating schools, our students put up a brilliant show in various IT events. Tejas Garg of Class X and Aditya Awana of Class XI bagged the First position in Robotics Manual. Sanjhana H. and Siddharth Dixit of Class XII won the Second Position in News Reporting while Rishit and Harman Singh of Class VII bagged the Second Prize in Frame by Frame. The School won the overall trophy on the basis of overall performance in the Fest. Principal, Mrs D. Kaur congratulated the students and the Computer Department on their achievement.
